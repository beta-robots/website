Skills: We face technological challenges by, first, applying a system design view point, and then using our tools and skills such as programming, algorithm design, sensor fusion, electronics and maths.

Applications: Robot design and prototyping, human-machine interfaces, visual/performing arts, intelligent furniture, domotics, custom automation, ...
